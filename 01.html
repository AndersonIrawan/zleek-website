User Research


In order to identify the needs of the targeted users, university students and potentially others, an interview was conducted towards 6 individuals to figure out their needs for the app. After data gathering, a persona was made based off the accumulated information of the different interviewees.

Additionally, to understand how the app would appear and function, a user journey map based off the user persona was created to identify points of struggle. The map was divided into 6 different swim lanes. Emojis were used to visualize the thoughts and emotions the user may experience.

Finally, to properly visualize the journey of the persona, a flow chart was made. This was a linear version from a broad version where more possibilities were considered for the other features the app may bring.

Intent Frames


The layout of the app was made by arranging shapes around a phone resolution. Each shape was then labelled to name its possible content. The app was set to consist of 5 different sections consisting of a Home, Community, Search, Save, and Profile page.

Wireframing


The contents of the app is layed out more intricately. Typography is incorporated to the wireframe to measure out how the texts fill up the space.

Graphic Design


The Freshipe logotype is the main branding identity of the app. It mainly consists of the two primary colors of the brand: Bright Orange (#EA4D28) and White. The size of the logotype may be scaled down to what ever size appropriate.

The main branding of Freshipe consists of two main font families: Pacifico and Inter. Pacifico is primarily used for the logotype, it may also be used to display some more playful text all over the app. Inter, on the other hand, is used for the rest of the typefaces across the app, as demonstrated below.

Aside from the two primary colors, Freshipe features other shades of the bright orange (#EA4D28) to bring forth and depth to the app. Due to the heavy use of white in the app, the additional yellow (#FFD747) is added to contrast items better on white backgrounds.
